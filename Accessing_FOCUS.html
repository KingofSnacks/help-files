Accessing FOCUS

From your device, go to training.parks.ca.gov using a supported browser Chrome, Edge, and Safari. Click on the FOCUS link at the right side under Related Pages navigation panel. Click on the system icon/logo to proceed to the FOCUS Welcome page.
